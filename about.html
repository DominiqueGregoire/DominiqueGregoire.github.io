Every woman's online fashion buying companion

How We Started

This all started with a couple of friends needing something new to wear for an important occasion. We looked online and found two gorgeous outfits, we checked the sizing and parted with our money. The outfits arrived a few days later ... we tried them on ... only to find that one was too big and the other looked dreadful on. After a few tears, a cocktail or two, and lots of discussions... Fit me Perfectly was born.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make sure that every woman can look her very best, by being able to purchase clothes online without having the dilemma of returning the items, selling them online, or giving them away. We believe that online shopping should be as easy as going to the store and trying on outfits. Which is where our avatar comes in
