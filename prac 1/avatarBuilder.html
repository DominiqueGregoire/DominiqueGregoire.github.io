Fit me Perfectly

Every woman's online fashion buying companion

Step 1

To build your avatar we require that you take and upload 4 photos of yourself in a bathing suit. This is so that we can tailor make your avatar to be as lifelike as possible. Once the photos have been put into our avatar builder, our AI uses the photos to create a lifelike representation of you. Once we have finished with the photos they are destroyed. This is to preserve your privacy. So if your body shape changes then you will have to adjust your avatar accordingly. We require front, back, and two side views. Please take your photos in natural light so that we can match your complexion and skin colour.

Step 2

Next, this is the hardest part, but we need to know your measurements. The usual hip, waist, bust and buttocks, as well as arm and thigh circumferences. This allows us to assist you in sizing. Unfortunately, every store has different sizing ... a medium in one store may be a small in another.


Once this is done we let our AI do its work and all you then have to do is link the shopping site with your avatar. Your avatar will then go into the dressing room and when she comes out you can see if the size you have chosen fits properly, if the clothing fits your body type and shape, and hides or emphasises the sections you want to hide or prefer.
